About Forge Downloader

Forge Downloader is an all-in-one media downloader designed to help you save online content quickly, safely, and without stress. What started as a YouTube-only downloader has now evolved into a multi-platform tool that allows users to download content from:

YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, X (Twitter), Pinterest and more platforms coming soon.

Whether you're a creator, student, editor, or casual viewer, Forge Downloader gives you the ability to download videos or audio for offline access, personal projects, learning, or inspiration.

Our mission is simple: make downloading content from across the internet easy, fast, and accessible to everyone.

We believe in convenience, simplicity, and respecting copyright guidelines. Forge Downloader is intended for personal use only. Please ensure you have the right to download any content you save.
